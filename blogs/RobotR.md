### Books Worth Owning – Factory Automation & Industry 4.0 (paid)
- [Factory Automation: The Definitive Guide](https://www.isa.org/products/factory-automation-2nd-edition) – Javier Silvestre-Blanes (ISA, 2022) – Comprehensive textbook covering PLCs, SCADA, MES, IIoT, and full automation pyramid integration
- [Industrial Automation and Process Control](https://www.isa.org/products/industrial-automation-and-process-control) – Jon Stenerson (ISA, 2020) – Practical reference used in industry training; strong on sensors, actuators, PID, DCS, and safety systems
- [The Smart Factory: How Industry 4.0 is Changing Manufacturing](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/distributed/S/bo214987582.html) – Detlef Zühlke (Hanser / University of Chicago Press, 2023) – Vision and implementation roadmap from the father of Germany’s Industrie 4.0 initiative
- [Implementing Industry 4.0: The Model Factory Approach](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783030518950) – Christian Leyh & Michael Fuchs (Springer, 2021) – Step-by-step guide with real model factory case studies from RWTH Aachen and Fraunhofer
- [Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES): Optimal Design, Planning, and Deployment](https://www.mhprofessional.com/manufacturing-execution-systems-mes-optimal-design-planning-and-deployment-9781260458971-usa) – Heiko Meyer et al. (McGraw-Hill, 2022) – Industry-standard reference for ISA-95, MOM, and modern MES architectures
- [Industrial Cybersecurity: Efficiently Secure Critical Infrastructure](https://www.isa.org/products/industrial-cybersecurity-second-edition) – Pascal Ackerman (ISA, 2021) – Practical Purdue Model defense, IEC 62443, OT incident response, and zero-trust for factories
- [The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement (30th Anniversary Edition)](https://www.northriverpress.com/the-goal-a-process-of-ongoing-improvement/) – Eliyahu M. Goldratt (North River Press) – Still the single most important book on constraint-based manufacturing thinking; required reading for any automation leader
- [Learning from the Factory Floor: Digital Transformation in Manufacturing](https://www.routledge.com/Learning-from-the-Factory-Floor-Digital-Transformation-in-Manufacturing/Hoffmann/p/book/9781032499819) – Andreas Hoffmann (Routledge, 2024) – Real-world lessons from 100+ digital factory projects across automotive, pharma, and consumer goods
- [Handbook of Industry 4.0 and SMART Manufacturing](https://www.crcpress.com/Handbook-of-Industry-40-and-SMART-Manufacturing/Chen-Khan/p/book/9781138600140) – Edited by Chao-Hsien Chu & Soundar Kumara (CRC Press, 2022) – 900+ page reference covering RAMI 4.0, digital twins, AI, and edge analytics
- [Smart Manufacturing: Concepts and Methods](https://www.elsevier.com/books/smart-manufacturing/matthews/978-0-12-820027-8) – Masoud Soroush et al. (Elsevier, 2020) – Deep dive into model-predictive control, real-time optimization, and cyber-physical production systems

### Books Worth Owning – Cost Calculation, ROI & Justification of Factory Automation / Industry 4.0 / CapEx Projects (paid)
- [Capital Investment & Valuation for Automation Projects](https://www.isa.org/products/capital-investment-and-valuation-for-automation-projects) – Nick Bararini (ISA, 2023) – The definitive ISA guide written specifically for automation engineers and plant managers; step-by-step NPV, IRR, payback, TCO, risk-adjusted ROI with real factory examples
- [Making the Business Case for Automation and Digital Transformation](https://www.mhprofessional.com/making-the-business-case-for-automation-and-digital-transformation-9781265595623-usa) – David B. Durocher & Gregory Wilcox (McGraw-Hill, 2024) – Practical templates, financial models, and case studies used by Fortune-500 manufacturers to get CapEx approved
- [The Financial Justification of Automation Projects](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783031044581) – Peter G. Martin & David A. Hunter (Springer, 2022) – From the creators of Real-Time Performance Management; shows how to quantify intangible benefits (flexibility, quality, risk reduction) in hard dollars
- [Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for Automation Systems](https://www.isa.org/products/total-cost-of-ownership-tco-for-automation-systems) – Bill Lydon & John S. Rinaldi (ISA, 2021) – Lifecycle costing workbook covering hidden costs: engineering, integration, training, downtime, cybersecurity, spares, and decommissioning
- [ROI in Automation and Digitalization Projects](https://www.hanserpublications.com/book/9781569909478) – Jürgen Kletti & Rainer Lassalle (Hanser, 2023) – German “Wirtschaftlichkeitsrechnung” approach translated; includes ready-to-use Excel models for MES, robotics, and IIoT projects
- [Project Valuation and Risk Analysis in Industrial Automation](https://www.crcpress.com/Engineering-Economics-and-Project-Management-for-Industrial-Automation/Rold%C3%A1n-Viloria/p/book/9781032517933) – José Roldán Viloria (CRC Press, 2024) – Monte Carlo simulation, real-options valuation, and sensitivity analysis tailored to robotics and smart-factory investments
- [The CapEx Playbook: How to Build Bulletproof Business Cases for Manufacturing Investments](https://www.independentpublisher.com/bookstore/product/the-capex-playbook/) – Scott DuFour (Independent Publisher, 2023) – CFO-approved frameworks used at GE, Toyota, and Siemens; includes automation-specific cash-flow models and executive presentation templates
- [Measuring and Managing the ROI of Digital Transformation](https://www.routledge.com/Measuring-and-Managing-the-ROI-of-Digital-Transformation-in-Manufacturing/Schuh-Geyer/p/book/9781032459479) – Günther Schuh & Volker Geyer (Routledge, 2024) – RWTH Aachen methodology with 40+ Industry 4.0 ROI case studies and maturity-based valuation models
- [Engineering Economics and Finance for Automation and Control Systems](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Financial+Management+and+Accounting+for+the+Automation+Professional-p-9781119687412) – Paul F. Lamb & Robert E. Lamb (Wiley-ISA, 2022) – Classic reference updated for OT projects; bridges the gap between engineers and finance departments
- [The Lean CFO: How to Deliver Value from Automation Investments](https://www.routledge.com/The-Lean-CFO-How-to-Deliver-Value-from-Automation-Investments/Watson/p/book/9781032499871) – Nicholas Katko (Routledge, 2023) – Lean accounting perspective on CapEx justification, throughput accounting, and avoiding traditional cost-accounting traps in automation projects
